About Milford India Spice

Welcome to Milford India Spice, where authentic flavors meet modern dining. Located in the heart of Milford, Massachusetts, we bring you the rich culinary traditions of India, Nepal, and Indo-Chinese cuisine.

🍛

Authentic Recipes

Traditional recipes passed down through generations, prepared with authentic spices and techniques.

🌶️

Fresh Ingredients

👨‍🍳

Expert Chefs

Our skilled chefs bring decades of experience in Indian, Nepalese, and Indo-Chinese cuisine.

Our Story

Founded with a passion for sharing authentic South Asian flavors, Milford India Spice has become a beloved destination for food lovers in the MetroWest area. Our commitment to quality, authenticity, and exceptional service has made us a trusted name in the community.

Whether you're craving the rich, creamy textures of our signature butter chicken, the aromatic spices of our tandoori specialties, or the comforting warmth of our traditional dal, we invite you to experience the true taste of India at Milford India Spice.
