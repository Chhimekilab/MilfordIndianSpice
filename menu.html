⚠️ IMPORTANT ALLERGEN INFORMATION

Our kitchen handles wheat, soy, eggs, milk, fish, shellfish, tree nuts, and peanuts. Cross-contamination may occur.

Please inform your server of any food allergies before ordering. While we take allergies seriously, we cannot guarantee allergen-free meals due to shared cooking surfaces and equipment.

Items marked with ❄️ can be made dairy-free upon request.

Items marked with 🔥 are extra spicy.

Management is not responsible for adverse reactions to foods consumed or items one may come in contact with while eating at this establishment.
